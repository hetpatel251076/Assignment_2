Why take a personal session of yoga?

It is common for new students to join a group yoga class and feel overwhelmed or out of place. Personal sessions provide direct one-on-one time with instructors to address any body issues.

- Perfect choice for beginners to yoga!

Beginners require clear explanations and patient instruction to learn the basics effectively. Those with injuries must also learn safe alignment techniques.

- Personal Online Yoga sessions also serve as therapy sessions!

These sessions are ideal for people with specific ailments. Our instructors will assess your condition and suggest a personalized yoga practice, including dietary recommendations and therapeutic sequences.
